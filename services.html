Services


Brenda Gray brings 25 years of experience in the roles of Controller, Business Manager, Director of Financial Services for small-to-mid size corporations and non-profits. Graysource Accounting was established to assist you with your bookkeeping and finances, utilizing Quickbooks desktop version or Quickbooks Online so that you can focus on running and growing your business.

Full-charge bookkeeping:
  • Utilize cloud or desktop based accounting
  • Clean-up of existing records, including entry of backlog of data
  • Accounts Payable - post invoices received from vendors, process payments, run monthly aging reports
  • Accounts Receivable - prepare and transmit invoices and statements to customers; input payments, prepare and run monthly aging reports
  • Payroll - maintain employees files, process checks or direct deposit, file federal and state employer tax reports; issue annual W-2s and 1099s
  • Monthly bank account and credit card reconciliations
Accounting:
  • Review monthly transactions, prepare journal entries with a knowledge of accrual method of accounting and prepaid/deferred revenue schedules
  • Prepare monthly reports - Income Statement, Balance Sheet, General Ledger, Cash Flow Statement, Fixed Assets Report with depreciation schedule
  • Interpret monthly results, review monthly financial information with owner/manager, develop customized reports, analyze economic trends, current and future financial obligations as well as current and projected income to meet future financial goals.
  • Budgeting and analysis - analyze historical performance, current spending and future plans; prepare budget; create weekly or monthly income statement reflecting actual expenses vs. budgeted expenses. Discuss with manager/owner ways to increase revenue and profitability
  • Cost Accounting - develop reports to effectively analyze profitability by client/customer, office/department and/or project
  • Prepare fiscal year-end closing entries to facilitate a clean audit and tax return preparation
Quickbooks Consulting:
  • New business accounting system set-up and customization
  • Existing system upgrade or data migration
  • Training

Contact us to discuss your needs. Working together we will develop a list of services that best fits your needs.
